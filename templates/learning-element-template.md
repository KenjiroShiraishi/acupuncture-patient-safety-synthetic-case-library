## Abstracted learning point

Describe the safety lesson in abstract form.

Example:

After acupuncture around the upper back or chest area, new respiratory symptoms should not automatically be explained as a normal post-treatment reaction. When pneumothorax or another adverse event cannot be excluded, the safer response is to recommend medical assessment.
